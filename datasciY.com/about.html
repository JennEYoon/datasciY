I am a self-taught Data Scientist. My focus is on the Python machine learning and deep learning ecosystem. I am currently studying Python data science full-time and am looking for full-time or freelance job opportunities as a data scientist or a data analyst. I am also a Derivatives Trading & Risk Consultant (MBA, Certified FRM). I was self-employed and provided derivatives and risk management consulting services to hedge funds and other financial entities. Other work experience includes the SEC as a Financial Analyst and an Economist, KPMG as a Senior Manager, NERA as a Senior Derivatives Analyst and Crescat Capital as an external Risk Management Consultant.

I received an MBA degree from the University of Chicago, Booth School of Business, and a Certified Financial Risk Manager (FRM) designation from GARP. I received a B.A. degree from Mount Holyoke College, with a major in economics and minors in physics and music. I received high honors in economics based on my research project & senior thesis, Innovation in the Computer Industry: with Case Studies.
